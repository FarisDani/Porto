MentorKita

Mentor Kita is a web-based platform developed by my team to help users find mentors tailored to their personal goals and interests.

Design with

Figma

Key Features

🎟️

Personalized Mentor Recommendations

The platform suggests mentors based on users’ interests, learning goals, and preferences.

💬

Advanced Search & Filtering

Users can easily find mentors by applying filters like category, rating, availability, and more.

🏆

Detailed Mentor Profiles

Each mentor profile includes a bio, skills, certifications, session availability, and feedback from past mentees.

Dashboard Page

Displays personalized mentor suggestions, recent interactions, and quick access to platform features.

Search Page

Enables users to browse and filter mentors based on expertise, experience, and goals.

Mentor Detail Page

Provides in-depth information about each mentor, including bio, skills, availability, and reviews.

Profile Page

Showcases individual profiles with editable personal data, session history, and user preferences.

Mentor Kita is the final capstone project of the ITC program, collaboratively developed by all UI/UX, Front-End, and Back-End teams. This project aims to create a comprehensive web-based platform that connects mentees with suitable mentors based on their goals and interests. Through close teamwork across all divisions, Mentor Kita delivers a user-centered experience with personalized mentor matching, advanced search features, and seamless scheduling—showcasing the full-stack capabilities and design skills of the entire group.
